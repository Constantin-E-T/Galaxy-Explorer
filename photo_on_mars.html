Fun Fact about Mars, Rover and NASA

Mars is the fourth planet from the sun and is known as the Red Planet. It's also the second closest planet to Earth after Venus.

NASA has sent several rovers to Mars to study the planet's geology and search for signs of past or present life. The first successful Mars rover was the Sojourner, which landed in 1996.
